HELLO WORLD!

MY NAME IS AARON.

"If it has electric currents running through it, then it falls under my jurisdiction."
--Aaron Hall

That's my motto when I'm at work, at home and all locations in between. Whether it's a computer, mobile device, printer, server or an electric blanket, when it breaks down, all eyes turn toward me to restore its usability...Immediately!

Luckily I enjoy such challenges--and most times, I have enough experience to reach success.

I've worked in many industries: a Chicago public school, food exporter, analytical marketing firm. While the technologies used may differ--some more advance than others--they have a common aspect: technology will breakdown.

And that's my entrance call.

Sure, I'm known as the fix-it guy. But it takes a bit more that a tech know-how to get things back in order. It's knowing how to work with people to calm them, engage them and help them understand that a technological breakdown is not an impending doom of civilization.

The secret sauce: Never let Curmudgeons get under your skin.
